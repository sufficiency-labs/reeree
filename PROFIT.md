## Sustainability Target

reeree costs $0 to operate (local models, no infrastructure). Sustainability = continued development time, which is self-funded as a tool Rob uses daily.

If reeree ever generates revenue (sponsorship, consulting), sustainability target is: covers its own development time so it doesn't compete with paid work for attention.

## What Success Looks Like

**Not:** Hockey stick growth, VC funding, millions of users.

**Yes:**
- Rob uses reeree daily and it makes him measurably faster
- A small community of terminal-native developers adopts it
- The dispatch console paradigm proves itself as an alternative to chatbot UX
- The project demonstrates VDSE in practice — every decision traces to a value
- It stays simple (< 5K lines) and maintainable by one person
